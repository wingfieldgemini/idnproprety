Bali's Most Coveted Areas

From beachfront sophistication to jungle tranquility, discover the perfect location for your lifestyle and investment goals

Seminyak

$800K - $5M+

The crown jewel of Bali's beach destinations, Seminyak epitomizes sophisticated tropical living. World-renowned beach clubs, Michelin-quality restaurants, and luxury boutiques create an atmosphere of refined elegance. Properties here command premium prices due to limited beachfront availability and established infrastructure.

Beach Clubs Fine Dining Shopping Nightlife

Canggu

$600K - $3M+

Where surf culture meets contemporary luxury, Canggu has rapidly evolved into Bali's most dynamic destination. Once rice fields, now home to world-class beach clubs, co-working spaces, and stunning modern villas. The area offers excellent growth potential with its young, international community.

Surfing Co-working Beach Clubs Growth Area

Ubud

$400K - $2M+

The cultural heart of Bali, Ubud offers a sanctuary of wellness and spirituality surrounded by emerald rice terraces and ancient temples. Properties here focus on harmony with nature, featuring sustainable design and breathtaking jungle or rice field views. Perfect for those seeking tranquility and cultural immersion.

Wellness Rice Fields Culture Yoga Retreats

Uluwatu

$1M - $8M+

Perched on dramatic limestone cliffs, Uluwatu represents the pinnacle of luxury in Bali. Spectacular ocean views, world-class surf breaks, and architectural marvels define this exclusive peninsula. Properties here are among Bali's most prestigious, offering unparalleled privacy and breathtaking natural beauty.

Clifftop Views Luxury Resorts Surf Breaks Exclusive

Sanur

$350K - $1.5M+

Bali's first beach resort area maintains its charm as a tranquil, family-friendly destination. Known for its peaceful beaches, established expatriate community, and relaxed atmosphere. Sanur offers excellent value for those seeking beachfront living without the crowds, making it ideal for permanent residence and rental investment.

Family Friendly Peaceful Expat Community Good Value

Nusa Dua

$600K - $4M+

A master-planned resort enclave featuring five-star hotels, championship golf courses, and pristine beaches. Nusa Dua represents Bali's most organized and secure area, with gated communities and world-class amenities. Perfect for those who prefer structured luxury living with easy access to international amenities.

5-Star Hotels Golf Course Gated Communities Security
