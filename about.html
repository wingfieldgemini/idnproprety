Our Story

IDN Property was founded in 2019 by a group of international investors who fell in love with Bali's unique charm and incredible investment potential.

Having navigated the complexities of foreign property ownership ourselves, we understand the challenges and opportunities that come with investing in Indonesia's paradise island. Our founders' firsthand experience as expat property owners gives us unique insight into what international clients truly need.

What started as helping friends and colleagues find their dream properties has evolved into Bali's most trusted boutique real estate agency. We've built our reputation on transparency, expertise, and genuine care for our clients' success.

Today, we're proud to be Bali specialists, focusing exclusively on the island's premium locations and luxury properties. Our deep local knowledge, combined with international standards of service, makes us the perfect bridge between global investors and Bali's extraordinary real estate opportunities.
